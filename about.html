Meet the Flock

Sunny Available for Adoption

Sunny is as gentle as his name suggests. This sweet boy is a little shy at first, but once he warms up to you, you’ll see his soft, affectionate side shine through. He loves soft-spoken voices, calm energy, and cozy perching spots by the window. If you're the kind of person who enjoys earning trust and building quiet bonds, Sunny will reward you with love, loyalty, and chirpy little chats.

Cherrie (Cher) Available for Adoption

Cher’s got big pineapple energy! Adventurous and curious just like her mama Sherbet, this girl is brave, bold, and fiercely independent. She's always the first to explore a new toy or fly across the room just because she can. She might be a little stubborn, but that just means she knows what she wants—and what she wants is a home that appreciates her spirit. If you're looking for a bird with spunk, sass, and a lot of love to give, Cher is your girl.

Baby Available for Adoption

The youngest of the flock and nearly a mirror image of his papa Einstein, Baby is a burst of joy wrapped in green and crimson feathers. Always sticking close to his twin brother Timmy, Baby is affectionate, curious, and always looking for new perches to conquer. He's just entering his playful prime and is ready to meet the person or family who’ll help him grow into a confident companion.

Timmy Healing - Not Available

Timmy may be small, but he’s full of heart. After a scary accident with a rope toy left him injured, Timmy was rushed to emergency care—through the rain, on a Sunday—and has been slowly healing ever since. His x-rays showed no breaks, but nerve damage may take time to fully heal. While he’s currently not available for adoption, he’s a testament to resilience and the deep care our flock receives.
